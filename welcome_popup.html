Welcome to the Organic Strawberry Farm Explorer

Thank you for exploring Conscious Kitchen’s Farm to School Project! This tool allows you to explore certified organic strawberry farms across California.

You can filter by county, check for GAP and CK certifications, and view key farm details including contact info.

For example: find GAP-certified farms in Santa Cruz that have worked with CK, and reach out directly using the farm table or interactive map.

Tips to get started:
✅ Use filters for county or certification
✅ Click strawberry icons on the map for farm info
✅ Browse the farm list for contact details
